THE "CARRY ON" FRANCHISE
(IN CHRONOLOGICAL ORDER)
[1958 - 1992]


CARRY ON COLUMBUS (1992): Voted "Worst British Film Ever Made" in a 2004 poll of British film actors, technicians, writers and directors of British cinema. Made 14 years after CARRY ON EMMANNUELLE (1978).


BLESS THIS HOUSE (1972) - Considered by many to be an unofficial CARRY ON film, due to it starring many of the same actors and produced by Peter Rogers and directed by Gerald Thomas, who Produced and Directed all of the CARRY ON films. This was actually a film version of a very popular British TV series, BLESS THIS HOUSE, that ran for six seasons (from 1971 to 1976).
